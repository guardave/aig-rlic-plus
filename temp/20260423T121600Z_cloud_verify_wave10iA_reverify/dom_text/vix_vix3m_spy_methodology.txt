Indicator Construction

VIX/VIX3M ratio. Computed as ^VIX / ^VIX3M at daily close. Ratio > 1 = backwardation (near-term fear dominates long-term hedging demand, signalling acute stress). Ratio < 1 = contango (the normal regime). Derived signals entered into the tournament:

Signal	Formula	Intent
ratio	VIX / VIX3M	raw ratio
ratio_z63	63-day z-score	short-window deviation
ratio_z126	126-day z-score	winner
ratio_z252	252-day z-score	annual z-score
ratio_rpct	rolling percentile rank	distribution-free signal
ratio_chg	day-over-day change	acceleration
backwardation	dummy (ratio > 1)	regime indicator

SPY daily adjusted closes from Yahoo Finance; forward returns computed in trading days at horizons h = 1, 5, 10, 21.
