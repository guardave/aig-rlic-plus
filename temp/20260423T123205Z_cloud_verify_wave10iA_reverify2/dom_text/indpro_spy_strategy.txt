The Strategy: Translating IP Signals into SPY Positioning

We tested hundreds of strategy combinations to find the most robust way to time SPY exposure using the Industrial Production signal.

Tournament Winner: S6_mom3m / P1_long_cash / LN/A

Tournament winner: Signal S6_mom3m (3-month IP momentum) / Threshold T1_fixed_p75 (75th percentile, fixed in-sample) / Strategy P1_long_cash (Long/Cash binary toggle) / Lead 6 months.

When the 3-month change in Industrial Production is above its historical 75th percentile (strong momentum), stay fully invested in SPY. Otherwise, move to cash. Apply the signal with a 6-month lead to account for IP's publication lag and the time it takes for the economy to respond.

How the Signal is Generated
Pull INDPRO. FRED series INDPRO, monthly SA. Released ~6 weeks after the reference month.
Compute 3-month momentum. mom3m_t = indpro_t / indpro_{t-3} - 1.
Fix the threshold once, in-sample. Take the 75th percentile of 3-month momentum from 1990-01 to 2017-12 and keep that number.
Apply the 6-month lead. Today's position is determined by the 3-month momentum from 6 months ago.
Translate to position. If the 6-month-lagged mom3m is above the fixed 75th-percentile threshold → long SPY (+1). Otherwise → cash (0). Rebalance monthly.
