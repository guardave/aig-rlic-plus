Indicator Construction

Building Permits (PERMIT). FRED series measuring new privately-owned housing units authorised each month, seasonally adjusted at annual rate (SAAR). Derived signals entered into the tournament:

Signal	Formula	Intent
permit	raw level	non-stationary; regime reference only
permit_yoy	(permit_t / permit_{t-12}) - 1	year-on-year growth
permit_mom	(permit_t / permit_{t-1}) - 1	1-month momentum — winner
permit_zscore	rolling 36M z-score of level	standardised deviation
permit_mom3m	3-month momentum	medium-horizon momentum
permit_mom6m	6-month momentum	lowest-frequency momentum

SPY daily adjusted closes (Yahoo Finance, auto_adjust=True) are resampled to monthly last close; forward returns are computed as spy.shift(-h) / spy - 1 for h = 1, 3, 6, 12 months.
