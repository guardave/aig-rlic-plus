Indicator Construction

Michigan Consumer Sentiment (UMCSENT). FRED series: monthly index of consumer confidence published by the University of Michigan Survey of Consumers. Index baseline = 100 (November 1966). Released mid-month for the current survey month. Approximately 500 households surveyed. Not seasonally adjusted (the survey methodology removes most seasonality at source).

Derived signals entered into the tournament:

Signal	Formula	Stationarity
umcsent	raw level	non-stationary (use transformed)
umcsent_yoy	(umcsent / umcsent.shift(12) − 1) × 100	winner — approximately stationary
umcsent_mom	1-month change	approximately stationary
umcsent_zscore	36M rolling z-score	stationary by construction
umcsent_3m_ma	3-month moving average	non-stationary (regime indicator)
umcsent_direction	sign(umcsent_mom)	stationary by construction
umcsent_dev_ma	level − 3M MA	approximately stationary

XLV target. Daily adjusted closing prices (Yahoo Finance, auto_adjust=True) resampled to monthly last close. Forward return series computed as xlv.shift(-h) / xlv - 1 for h = 1, 3, 6, 12 months.
